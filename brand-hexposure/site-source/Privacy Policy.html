Privacy Policy

We built Hexposure to help you understand your external exposure — not to expose yours. This policy explains exactly what data we collect, why, and how it's handled.

Plain-language summary: We collect your work email to manage waitlist and beta access. When you add domains, we scan them externally — we never ask for internal access, credentials, or agent installs. We don't sell your data. Ever.

01 Who we are

Hexposure is an external attack-surface management platform operated by Hexius ("we," "us," or "our"). Hexposure is a product of Hexius. References to "Hexposure," "the platform," or "the service" in this policy all refer to the product and services provided by Hexius.

Our website is hexposure.ca and our main company website is hexius.com. Questions about this policy can be directed to the contact details at the end of this document.

02 Information we collect

Information you give us

  • Email address — collected when you join the waitlist or register for beta access. We use work emails only.
  • Domain names — the external domains and subdomains you ask us to scan on your behalf.
  • Account details — name, company name, and role, if you provide them during onboarding.
  • Communications — any messages you send us via email or in-product feedback forms.

Information generated by the service

  • Scan results — findings, asset inventories, and health scores produced by our external scans of your domains. This data belongs to you.
  • Usage data — pages visited, features used, and actions taken inside the platform, collected to improve the product.
  • Log data — IP addresses, browser type, and timestamps associated with your sessions, collected automatically by our servers.

03 How we use it

  • Deliver the service — run scans, generate reports, and show you your dashboard.
  • Manage access — process waitlist signups and send invite and onboarding emails.
  • Communicate with you — send scan notifications, product updates, and important policy changes. You can opt out of marketing emails at any time.
  • Improve the platform — understand how the product is used and fix issues.
  • Legal compliance — meet obligations under applicable law.

We do not use your data to train AI or machine-learning models. We do not sell your data to any third party.

04 Sharing & disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data only in these limited situations:

  • Service providers — trusted vendors who help us operate the platform (hosting, email delivery, analytics). Each provider is bound by a data processing agreement and may not use your data for their own purposes.
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, court order, or regulatory authority, we may disclose information as necessary.
  • Business transfer — if Hexius is acquired or merges with another entity, your data may transfer as part of that transaction. We will notify you before that happens.
  • With your consent — any other sharing requires your explicit permission.

05 Scan & asset data

When you add a domain to Hexposure, we perform an external, unauthenticated scan — the same kind anyone on the internet could perform. We do not install agents, request credentials, access internal systems, or touch anything behind your firewall.

The resulting asset inventory and findings are your data. We process them to generate your health score and reports. We do not share your scan results with other customers or use them to benchmark your company against others in any identifiable way.

Scan data is stored for the duration of your account plus a 90-day post-closure grace period, after which it is permanently deleted.

06 Retention

  • Waitlist emails — kept until you unsubscribe, convert to a beta user, or request deletion.
  • Account data — kept for the life of your account plus 90 days after closure.
  • Scan & report data — kept for the life of your account plus 90 days after closure.
  • Log data — retained for up to 12 months for security and debugging purposes.

You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing us at the address below.

07 Your rights

Depending on where you're located, you may have rights including:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion — ask us to delete your personal data.
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection — object to our processing of your data for marketing purposes.
  • Withdrawal of consent — where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@hexposure.ca. We will respond within 30 days.

08 Cookies & tracking

We use a minimal set of cookies to operate the platform:

  • Session cookies — keep you signed in during a browsing session.
  • Preference cookies — remember your theme preference (dark/light mode). Stored locally in your browser.
  • Analytics — we may use a privacy-focused analytics tool that does not fingerprint users or track across sites. No advertising or retargeting cookies are used.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Disabling session cookies will prevent you from signing in.

09 Security

We apply security-by-default practices to protect your data: encrypted connections (TLS), access controls, and regular review of our own external surface (we use our own product).

No system is perfectly secure. If you discover a security issue, please contact us at security@hexposure.ca before disclosing publicly.

10 Children

Hexposure is a B2B security platform intended for business use only. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

11 Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will notify you by email before the change takes effect. Continued use of the platform after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
