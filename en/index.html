Attack paths

One critical breach is rarely one bug.

Separate findings look harmless on their own. Hexposure connects them into the exact route an attacker would take — and your score weights the chain, so you cut it at the weakest link.

The loop

From signup to fixed, in four steps.

Your first scan starts the moment your domains are verified. After that we re-run it every month and re-check your fixes automatically.

01

Discover

We enumerate every subdomain, IP, service, endpoint and certificate tied to your domains — building a live inventory.

02

Test

Each asset is probed from the outside: open ports, weak TLS, exposed panels, leaked secrets, stray files, look-alikes.

03

Rank

Findings are weighted by severity, exploitability and business impact into one score and a ranked priority list.

04

Fix & re-check

You work the list with step-by-step guidance; the next scan confirms each fix automatically.
