The platform

One system that sees, ranks and proves.

Hexposure runs a continuous loop across your external surface. It starts by seeing everything, weighs what it finds by business risk, and ends with a health score you can defend. Here's each job, up close.

VisibilityBusiness riskAssurance
01 — Visibility

See everything you expose.

Most tools only check the assets you already know about. Hexposure starts from the outside and discovers the rest — then keeps a living inventory that updates every month.

The forgotten staging box, the leftover backup, the third-party subdomain, the look-alike registration: if it's reachable from the internet, it's on your map.

  • Subdomains, IPs & live services enumerated continuously
  • App endpoints & exposed files that scanners walk past
  • Certificates & look-alike domains tracked over time

02 — Business risk

Know what's actually at stake.

A wall of "criticals" isn't a plan. Hexposure weighs every exposure by exploitability and business impact — customer data, revenue, reputation — and chains related findings into attack paths.

What you get back isn't 765 findings. It's the handful that matter, in order, each with a plain-English "why it matters" and a step-by-step fix.

  • Impact-weighted ranking not scanner severity
  • Chained attack paths fix one, collapse many
  • Step-by-step remediation for every item

03 — Assurance

Prove you're covered.

Everything resolves into one health score — weighted by business risk, moving as you fix things. Because it's built on far more visibility than a ratings vendor, it's a number you can actually defend.

Behind it sits a board-ready report and a named human advisor who knows your environment and joins your reviews. Assurance, not a vanity grade.

  • A score that trends down, quarter over quarter
  • Board-ready report generated, not hand-assembled
  • Dedicated advisor on the line when you need one
