Questions

The things teams ask first.

Do I need to install anything?
No. Hexposure works entirely from the outside — the same vantage point an attacker has. There are no agents, no code changes, and no internal access. You verify your domains and we take it from there.
How is this different from a security rating?
Ratings infer a grade from passive signals and leave you to interpret it. Hexposure actively discovers and tests far more of your surface, ranks findings by business risk, tells you exactly what to fix, and gives you a score that moves as you do.
What do I actually get?
A living inventory of everything you expose, a ranked short list of what to fix first with step-by-step guidance, attack-path analysis, a trended health score, a board-ready report, and a named security advisor.
How often does it run?
Your first scan begins as soon as your domains are verified. After that, Hexposure re-scans every month and automatically re-checks your fixes, so your inventory and score stay current.
